Payment Terms

Payments will be made to a traveler when all the following conditions are met: 1) Confirmation from traveler about successful delivery of shipment(s) 2) Confirmation from the sender(s) about the successfully delivery of shipment(s) 3) After elapsing a clearance period of 15 days upon receiving confirmation from traveler about successful delivery of shipment(s). Payments will be made in the form of electronic funds transfer or any other method that Shippers chooses to at a future time.

Shippers collects a transaction fee of 30% per transaction. For example, a $100.00 transaction will result in a net revenue of $70.00 (= $100 - (30% of $100)) and total payment of $70.00 will be paid out to you.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

If an order is cancelled (for any reason) or if the shipment delivery cannot be confirmed (either by the sender or by the traveler), the payment funds will be returned to the Sender. Shippers will not refund payments made for cancelled shipments back to your credit card account or other account. Funds from delivery refunds are returned to the Sender's balance and are available for future shipment made on Shippers App.
