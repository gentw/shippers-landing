How Shippers App Works

  • Senders
  • Travelers

Shippers is the go-to way to shop for products that aren’t available in your country or are too expensive to buy locally. You can order just about anything on Shippers, from baby clothes and kids toys to tech gadgets and nutritional supplements.

Create a shipment request

Tell us about the item you wish to send. Include the details like size, weight, where you want it to be delivered and how much you are willing to pay.

Or, look for travelers going your way

Browse our community of available travelers and search for traveler heading to your location and make your offer on a selected traveler

Agree on the fee you are paying to your traveler

Whether you selected the traveler from the community or a traveler sent a request to carry your shipment, negotiate and agree on the price and confirm the trip or request. Our chat feature will ensure a smooth and fast communication and negotiation process between you and the traveler.

Wait for your item to be delivered

Agree on a time and location to meet your traveler and receive your shipment. Once you receive your item, confirm the delivery and make the payment to the traveler via the app.

Add a new trip

Travel, share your luggage space and make money. Include details like how maximum item/s weight and size you can carry and your asking rate per kg.

Or, browse shipment requests

Find an order you can deliver and make a bid. Search for shipment requests based on your next destination and make an offer on how much you want to get paid for delivering the item.

Confirm shipment with the sender

Once bid is accepted by sender or you accept a shipment request from a sender, finalize the details on where you will pick up the item and check the item thoroughly before you pack with your luggage.

Deliver the item and get paid

Arrange for time and location with the sender to deliver the package. Once sender received and confirmed their order, we’ll make a payment to your account on file.
